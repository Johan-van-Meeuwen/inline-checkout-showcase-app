Paddle Checkout Demo Builder

This app enables all Paddlers to create realistic inline and overlay checkout experiences with a prospect's or customer's branding in under five minutes.

A step-by-step guide will beavailable in the future here, but for now, if you've any questions, reach out to Johan.

Fill out the form below to create a product and a price for that product, add a company and product logos, their primary brand colour and their pre-checkout pricing page. Add an additional product and price if you want to showcase multi-product checkouts.

Wait up to 60 seconds after submitting the form and be redirected to your customised checkout journeys.

3. Enter primary brand colour

To get this, go to your seller's website, using a colour picker (here's one if you don't have one), click on the primary colour of the seller's website and copy the hexcode. It will be 3-8 characters with a # at the start (e.g. #fddd36).
